Buffalo Chicken


Reference Video

Description

Some pretty good buffalo chicken that is quick and easy to make.

This recipe is for 1 serving but I typically but enough to make 5 days worth. I also usually make veggies along with something like fries, potato wedges, or rice to go with this.

Ingredients

  • Boneless Skinless Chicken Breast [2.5 lbs] (1135 g)
  • Buffalo Sauce [2-3 Tbsp] (30 ml)
  • Olive Oil [1 Tbsp] (15 g)
  • Butter [1 Tbsp] (14 g)]
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Start preheating Oven to 400°F
  2. Cut the chicken breast into one inch cubes.
  3. Season lightly with salt and pepper.
  4. Lightly spray a large sheet pan with butter.
  5. Bake at 400°F for 10-12 minutes, flipping halfway.
    1. (Optional) Use a skillet instead.
    2. Heat a large skillet over medium high heat and add in 1 Tbsp of olive oil.
    3. Add the chicken and cook until it is white in the center, around 7-8 minutes.
    4. When the chicken is just about cooked through, melt 1 Tbsp of butter and mix in 1 tsp of cornstarch and 2-3 Tbsp of buffalo sauce.
  6. Make Sauce.
    1. Mix 1 Tbsp of melted butter and 2-3 Tbsp of buffalo sauce.
    2. Stir.
  7. After cooking chicken, take out and put in a large bowl to mix with sauce. Add more sauce if needed.
  8. Serve or put in the fridge/freezer.
    • (Optional) Garnish with some parsley.

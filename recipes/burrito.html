Burrito

Description

Some great burritos that are filling and tasty. They are made to have lots of protein and not too high in calories.
This recipe is for 1 serving but I typically make however many I can with the amount of meat I purchased, freezing any burritos I won't eat within ~4 days.

Ingredients

  • 10''/25cm Wheat Tortilla [x6]
  • Ground beef (or chicken/turkey) [2 lbs] (900g)
  • Yellow Onion [250g]
  • Sieved or puree canned tomato [300g]
  • Laughing cow wedges [x4]
  • Shredded mozzarella [120g]
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Oregano
  • Garlic powder
  • (Optional) Salsa Verde

Steps

  1. With 300g of canned tomato; add ~1 tbsp. Salt, ~1.5 tbsp. Black pepper, ~2 tsp. Oregano then Mix.
  2. Dice onions.
  3. Use a big pan with oil on medium-high.
  4. Add beef for 4-5 minutes. While cooking add some salt and black pepper as needed along with ~1 tsp. of garlic powder.
  5. Stir occasionally until mostly brown then add chopped onion, continue to stir.
  6. After ~11 minutes add tomato sauce then stir well.
  7. Add 4 cheese wedges, stirring for a few minutes until cheese is melted in with beef.
  8. Now add 120g of shredded mozzarella and stir again until melted.
  9. (Optional) Add to plate and let cool in fridge or on counter so the hot beef does not cause tortillas to become soggy and weak.
  10. Lay burrito on aluminum foil (optionally also use parchment paper to make reheating better) then add ~250g of meat on each tortilla and roll up burrito then wrap burrito in aluminum foil.
  11. Put burritos in the fridge or in a ziploc bag then in the freezer.
  12. (Optional) Serve with Salsa Verde.

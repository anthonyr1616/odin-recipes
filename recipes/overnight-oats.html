Overnight Oats


Reference Video

Description

If you don't have time to make breakfast in the mornings, Overnight Oats are a meal prep game-changer. Healthy, delicious, and super easy to prep - you can have a work week's worth of breakfast prepared in minutes. I especially love them because they keep me feeling full for hours.
This recipe is for 1 serving but I typically make 5 jars of Overnight Oats to eat throughout the week.

Ingredients

  • Rolled Oats [½ cup]
  • Chia Seeds [1 tbsp.]
  • Milk [½ cup]
  • Greek Yogurt [¼ Cup]
  • Maple Syrup (or Honey) [1 tbsp.]
  • Mason Jar(s)

Steps

  1. Add ½ cup of rolled oats.
  2. Add 1 tbsp. Chia Seeds.
  3. Add ½ cup of milk.
  4. Add ¼ cup of greek yogurt.
  5. Add 1 tbsp. of maple syrup.
  6. Give it a good stir.
  7. Put in the fridge for ~3 hours or overnight.
  8. Serve.
    • (Optional) Add Toppings like Berries, Granola, and Nuts
